So what do you need to do to get your license?

  • The Theory Test


    • Apply online at www.theorytest.ie
    • The current fee for this is €45
    • A public services card is required, you can get one here

  • The Learners Permit

    • You can apply for your theory test here
    • This permit costs €35
    • It will take approximately one week to arrive

  • The IBT (Initial Basic Training)

    • You can find an ibt instructor near you here

  • The Driving Test

    • The driving test can be completed 6 months after you have received your learners permit
    • The cost of sitting the test is €85
    • It will take approximately one week to arrive
    • You can apply for your driving test here
