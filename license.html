Motorcycle License Categories

Category Vehicle type Minimum age of driver
AM Mopeds and light quadricycles 16 years
A1 Motorcycles with an engine capacity not exceeding 125 cubic centimetres, with a power rating not exceeding 11 kW and with a power to weight ratio not exceeding 0.1 kW/kg. Motor tricycles with a power rating not exceeding 15 kW. 16 years
A2 Motorcycles of 35kW or less, with a maximum power/weight ratio of 0.2 kW/kg, and not derived from a vehicle of more than double its power. 18 years
A Motorcycles Motor tricycles 20 years (progressive access) 24 (Direct access) 21 Motor tricycles

So what does this all mean?

To but it simply, although there are some exceptions to this, the categories are as follows:


  1. AM: Mopeds up to 50cc
  2. A1: Bikes and Mopeds up to 125cc
  3. A2: Bikes and Mopeds up to 500cc
  4. A: Any displacement of Bike or Moped
